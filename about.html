About Me

Hey All,

My name is Casey Baker, and I am a Front-End Developer focused on providing clean, reliable user experiences. I entered the tech field in 2020, when just about everyone was trying to figure out what their real passions and career goals were. Although computers and fine arts have always been a huge part of my life, I had never taken those creative and technical skills into the professional world. Once I completed some schooling in Tech Support, I began leaning towards Quality Assurance testing for software in my current job. This engrained in me the importance of enjoyable and reliable interfaces, and made me realize that I could be the one creating those experiences for others. Fast-forward through several courses of Front-End Development workshops/school and here we are!

Preferred Editor: Visual Studio Code
Tools: Bootstrap, ReactJS, Responsive development.
